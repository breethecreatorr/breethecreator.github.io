BBP (BLACK. BUSINESS. PROMOTION)

BBP or better known as Black Business Promotion brings awareness to the skillful black business owners we have today. Every two weeks Bree Kelly selects a black business owner, whether it is a designer, rapper, or an artist, and she show cases them.
