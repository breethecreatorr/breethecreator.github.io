ABOUT Bree

Brianna Kelly is an indie soul sister that applies simplicity to every art. Ms. Kelly is a jack of all traits. She is a writer, videographer, photographer, graphic design artist, and innovator. Bree Kelly directs and edits music videos, as well as short documentaries and film and has a deep love for hip hop culture. Brianna Kelly ultimate goal is to become the next female Spike Lee. This means writing, directing, and also producing movies and eventually owning her own production company.

 

Bree Kelly has worked side by side with legendary producer, and creator of 40 Acres and a Mule, Monty Ross on a short film entitled "Wiggins, He Know not what he do."  Her trailer, "The Black Holocaust" have also been featured on The Washington Post, and received several awards. Bree Kelly was sent to Cannes, France to be an executive assistant for foreign films for Myriad Pictures. Bree Kelly has done it all. recently she was a casting assistant for Winsome Sinclair's WSA Casting. Now, Bree Kelly is a film intern for the number one digital website The HYPE Magazine.

 

When it comes to Bree Kelly it’s not the sky that’s the limit; it’s more like stars, moons, and the galaxy.
